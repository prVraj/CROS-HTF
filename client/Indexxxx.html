Frequently Asked Questions

What is a contactless restaurant ordering system?

+

A contactless restaurant ordering system allows customers to browse menus, place orders, and make payments using their own mobile devices without physical interaction with restaurant staff or menus.

Is it secure to make payments through a contactless ordering system?

+

Yes, most contactless ordering systems utilize secure payment gateways and encryption protocols to ensure the security of customer transactions.

Can customers still receive assistance if needed?

+

Yes, while the system reduces the need for direct interaction, restaurant staff are still available to provide assistance and answer any questions customers may have.

How does the system handle special requests or dietary restrictions?

+

Customers can usually add special requests or specify dietary restrictions directly through the ordering interface. This information is then communicated to the kitchen along with the order.
